About Jody

Jody uses an iPad and wants to join conversations around her. Our goal is to make participation smoother, faster, and less tiring.

Jody’s current situation

Communication challenges

Jody understands well, but several factors make it hard to speak and be heard in time:

  • Speaking can be slow or tiring, especially in noisy or stressful settings.
  • Existing tablet tools require many precise taps, menus, and navigation steps.
  • She often depends on someone else to quickly say a short phrase or answer a question.
What our solution aims to do

How we want to help

The solution supports Jody’s voice, not replaces it. We want to make it easier for her to join and follow conversations.

  • Provide a simple and fast way for Jody to be heard from her iPad.
  • Make common daily phrases easy to access and play when she needs them.
  • Reduce effort so she can focus on the interaction itself.
  • Increase her autonomy and social participation in different environments.
